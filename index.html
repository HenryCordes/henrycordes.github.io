Video

Sanne is an artist with an extraordinary voice that introduces people into her dreamlike world. Her 'soft' and sensitive songs show her vulnerability and the movie that is playing on the silver screen in her mind.

This young artist secretly is a bit stubborn, but knows exactly what she wants. Inspired by Adele, Lana del Rey and Billie Eilish she has already released several covers. Sanne writes her own lyrics, devises her own videos and has a contrary form of creativity.

Music

In recent years Sanne has performed with different bands and she has touched a lot of people with her voice. Sanne does everything by feeling and now she is ready to release her own music. She is telling her own story and lets people experience her quirky view on the world.

March 19th 2020 her single 'I Wanna Be You' was released, it is available on all streaming services like Spotify, Apple Music, etc.
